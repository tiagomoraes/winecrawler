Re-live: FIFA Women's World Cup closing press conference

Wednesday, 03 Jul 2019

A month of spectacular football is behind us, with 50 of the 52 matches at the FIFA Women's World Cup France 2019™ now played, showcasing some of the best players in the women's game. FIFA and the Local Organising Committee held a closing press conference on Friday 5 July 2019 in order to summarise the most important facets of the tournament and to prepare for the last two matches of the competition. You can follow the entire event via our live stream. **Participants:** * Gianni Infantino - FIFA President * Noël Le Graët - President of the French Football Federation (FFF) * Brigitte Henriques - Vice-President of the FFF and the Local Organising Committee

FIFA Women's World Cup 2019™

 Re-live: FIFA Technical Study Group Press Conference

Re-live: FIFA Techni...

Re-live: FIFA Technical Study Group Press Conference

Wednesday, 03 Jul 2019

The FIFA Technical Study Group (TSG) press conference took place at the Stade de Lyon on Friday 5 July 2019. You can re-live the entire event in the video above. The TSG is comprised of a selection of well-known experts who have analysed every game at the FIFA Women's World Cup France 2019™ and who have identified possible trends to include in the technical report that will be shared with all FIFA member associations after the competition ends. At the press conference, the TSG panel will provide insight on the opinions formed from the games at France 2019 so far. **Participating FIFA TSG members**: * April Heinrichs (Head, USA) * Sun Wen (China PR) * Nadine Kessler (Germany) * Elisabeth Loisel (France) * Clementine Toure (Côte d’Ivoire)
